THIRUVANANTHAPURAM



PADMANABHASWAMY TEMPLE

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple is one of the famous places to visit. This holy shrine is one of the 108 abodes of Lord Vishnu. A large number of devotees throng the temple on multiple occasions.

Location: 2 km from the main market of Trivandrum

Timings: 3:30 am to 4:45 am, 6:30 am to 7 am, 8:20 am to 10 am, 10:30 am to 11:10 am and 11:45 am to 12 pm, 5 pm to 6:15 pm and 6:45 pm to 7:20 pm

Entry fee: None

VIZHINJAM LIGHTHOUSE

The lighthouse was constructed in 1925, the 36 m high masonry tower is at an elevation of 57 m from sea level. There are 144 twisting steps and a steel ladder to reach the top. From the top, one can witness the stunning views of the Arabian Sea on one side and the umbrella of coconut trees on the other.

Location: 10 km away from Thiruvananthapuram

Timings: 3 PM to 6 pm

Entry fee: INR 10/- for Indians and 25/- for foreigners

SHANGHUMUKHAM BEACH

Shanghumukham Beach is an ideal place for those seeking peace and serenity. Far from the hustle and bustle of the city, this place is known for breath-taking sunrises and sunsets; visit to this beach is one of the best things to do in Trivandrum The calm environs and exquisite shoreline here is undoubtedly commendable.

Location: 10 kilometers from Trivandrum Central

Timings: No timing restrictions

Entry fee: None

VARKALA BEACH

This secluded beach is situated 54 kms north of Trivandrum. An exemplary escape from the bustling crowd of the city, Varkala is a paradise in its own right. The beach ensures a refreshing vacation with spectacular landscape, multiple international cuisines and mesmerising sunset view.

Location: 54 km to the north of Thiruvananthapuram

Timings: No timing restrictions

Entry fee: None

PONMUDI

The captivating hill station of Ponmudi is at a distance of 61 kms from Trivandrum. It is situated 915 meters above sea level and offers respite from the heat and hustle and bustle of other cities. Its narrow, meandering pathways, dark woods and soothing climate make it a splendid location to spend a holiday at.

Location: 61 kms from Trivandrum

Timings: No timing restrictions

Entry fee: None

KUTHIRAMALIKA PALACE MUSEUM

Located adjacent to Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kuthiramalika Palace Museum is the rarest specimen of workmanship. Built by Maharaja Swathi Balarama Varma, this palace displays valuable collections of royal family.

Location: 2 kilometers from Trivandrum Central

Timings: 8.30 AM to 1.00 PM and 3.00 PM to 5.30 PM

Entry fee: Rs. 10 for Indians, Rs. 3 for kids, Rs. 20 for foreigners including kids and Rs. 15 for the camera
